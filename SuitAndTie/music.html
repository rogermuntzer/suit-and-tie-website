SUIT & TIE

Live events music

London based three-piece band Suit & Tie are perfect for weddings, parties and events. 10+ years of gigging experience ensures the highest quality of performance and professionalism for your special occasion.

With sharp vocals and a tight set, they can tailor their track list to fill any dancefloor, hall or even field. They will give you a night you will never forget.

Based in London, Suit & Tie perform mainly in the South East of England, but are willing to travel.

Suit & Tie are a fun, fresh, energetic pop-rock, funk and soul covers band.

The band provide their own PA and lighting, as well as a complimentary DJ service.

With a repertoire list that mixes classic songs of yesterday with modern anthems, this band will have something for everyone and will ensure all your guests have a night to remember!
